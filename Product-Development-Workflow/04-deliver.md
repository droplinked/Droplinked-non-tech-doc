## â“ FAQ

**Q: What if a test fails?**  
A: The release moves to "Test Failed" status, and a bug is created in Maintain. The affected task goes back to Build for fixing.

**Q: Who approves the release?**  
A: Product Manager or Tech Lead gives final approval.

**Q: How long should staging take?**  
A: Typically 1-2 days for stakeholder review.

**Q: What happens after "Delivered"?**  
A: The release is closed. Any issues found go to the Maintain board as bugs.
