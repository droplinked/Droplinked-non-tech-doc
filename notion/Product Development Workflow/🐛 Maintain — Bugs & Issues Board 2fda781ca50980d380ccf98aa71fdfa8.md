## â“ FAQ

**Q: Who can report bugs?**

A: Anyone â€” QA, developers, users, stakeholders.

**Q: What if a bug is actually a feature request?**

A: Close the bug and create a Request in the Intake folder.

**Q: How are bugs prioritized?**

A: By severity (Critical > High > Medium > Low) and business impact.

**Q: Do all bugs need fix tasks?**

A: Yes, every confirmed bug should have a linked fix task in Build.

**Q: What about duplicate bugs?**

A: Close the duplicate and link to the original bug.
